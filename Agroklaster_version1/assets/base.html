hello

East Coast-based Web Developer Cay is a keen observer driven by an innate curiosity and the desire to establish intimate connection with her subjects. Guided by her intuition rather than working with premeditated concepts, the silence and simplicity of her work explores notions of solitude and stillness in everyday moments. Distilled to their most essential form, her images are capable of expressing and evoking subtly nuanced emotions.

This charismatic developer—when she’s not out being a foodie—can often be found updating her blog, informing her reader on her latest fashion finds and style ideas. A woman of many hats.
